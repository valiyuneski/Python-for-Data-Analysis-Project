3c · Visualizations

City MPG histogram: Right-skewed distribution with most cars clustered at 15–25 MPG. A small tail of hybrids/electrics extends toward 100+ MPG.
MSRP by Size: Large vehicles command a ~51% premium over Compact cars, reflecting the dominance of luxury SUVs and full-size trucks in that segment.
HP vs MSRP scatter: Clear positive trend — more powerful engines generally mean higher prices. Wide spread at high HP indicates that brand prestige also plays a major role.
Boxplot: RWD and AWD vehicles show significantly higher median MSRPs and wider IQRs, reflecting more varied luxury and performance offerings in those drivetrain categories.
